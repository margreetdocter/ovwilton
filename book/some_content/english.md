# English
OV Wilton can be characterized as follows:
- We are making music at a the highest level of Dutch symphonic concert band. ;
- There is a concert band, woodwind trio and dance band;
- We only do stage appearances;
- The repertoire is diverse, from classic to modern/ pop, with just our concert band or solist(s) & choir(s)
- 
Above all, we’re just a friendly group of people who love making music together, and we welcome anyone with a decent playing level who wants to join.  If you are in/near Schiedam only for a limited period of time (some months, some years) you are welcome!

Contact us, to join one of our rehearsals. 

Unfortunately, this page is the only one in English. Please browse through the menu, especially the photos and sound (beeld & geluid) pages, to get a better impression of who we are.
